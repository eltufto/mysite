DICKIE'S GINGER

Est. 2014

Made in Vancouver

"I can't find a decent Ginger Beer anywhere"


The complaint that started it all.

What to do when nothing is good enough? Make your own!

After many months of recipes, taste tests, and a lot of ginger, Dickie's Ginger is ready. This is a stripped down and simple take on ginger beer using only the best ingredients.

What goes in:

Real Ginger

Never any artificial flavours or extracts

Cane Sugar

No corn syrup or artificial sweeteners

Fresh Lemons

A match made in gingery heaven

That's it. Nice and simple.

Ginger Beer Perfection
